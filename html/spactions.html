Environmental Sustainability in SP
Biodiversity of Plants
on Campus
SP's campus is home to roughly 230 different species of plants! The presence of nature allows us to feel relaxed and helps calm us down when we are stressed. It also improves the quality of our air.
Smart Sensors
Most of the facilities on campus are equipped with smart sensors that monitors motion and air quality. For example, lights along T7's corridor have motion sensors. This allows the lights to be turnt on only when it is necessary and reduces electricity wastage.
Sustainable Buildings
SP houses 4 Green Mark Platinum Buildings. (T18A, T18B, T3B and the SP Sports Arena) Green mark's building rating system evaluates its environmental impact and performance. There are on-going efforts are being made to certify all 22 buildings in SP by 2025.
SP pledged to become a green campus, reduce its environmental impact and nurture people of sustanability.
SP believes in creating safe and green spaces for the well-being of its community and actively involves its community in activities regarding sustainability.
