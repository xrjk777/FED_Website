Want to find out more about Singapore Polytechnic or School of Computing?
Singapore Polytechnic is the oldest polytechnic in Singapore. It offers over 37 full-time diploma courses to prepare students for university and work. SP also offers 100+ different CCAs, you are sure to find a CCA that you enjoy.
School of Computing offers 4 diploma courses, Applied AI & Analytics, Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics, Information Technology and Common ICT Programme. Through these courses, you develop your skillsets through immersive, hands-on projects to enhance your knowledge and experience, giving you relevant knowledge in the IT field.
